Welcome to WhiskeyTab

Spaces & Collections

The hierarchy is simple:

  • 1
    Spaces

    High-level contexts like "Work", "Personal", or "Project X". Each space has its own independent list of collections.

  • 2
    Collections

    Folders within a space. This is where your actual tabs live.

  • 3
    Cards (Tabs)

    Individual bookmarks/tabs. You can drag and drop them to reorder.

Smart Sorting

Chaos in your tab strip? The Smart Sort feature (beta) helps you organize instantly.

1. Enter Smart Sort Mode

Click the Sort Icon (Arrow Up/Down) in the header. Tabs will visually reorder in the dashboard preview. This is safe‚Äînothing moves in your browser yet.

2. Apply Changes

If you like the new order, click the Checkmark button. Only then will your actual browser tabs be rearranged.

Duplicate Finder

WhiskeyTab scans not just for exact URL matches, but for "Smart Matches" too‚Äîignoring tracking IDs that make identical pages look different.

How it works

Click the Duplicate Finder button in the sidebar (or top right).
We'll show you groups of identical tabs. You can close all duplicates with one click, keeping just the one you need.

Smart Ignore

We automatically ignore tracking parameters like utm_source or fbclid.
example.com?ref=twitter == example.com

Configuration

Need to ignore custom params? Go to Settings > Search > Duplicate Finder Ignored Parameters to add your own comma-separated list.

Stashing Tabs

Overwhelmed? Clear your browser window in one click.

How to Stash

  1. Open the Sidebar.
  2. Click the "Stash All" button (icon looks like a box).
  3. All current open tabs will be saved into a new collection named "Stash [Date]".
  4. Your window is now clean and ready for a fresh start!

FAQ

Does WhiskeyTab sync across devices?
Not yet. Currently, WhiskeyTab is local-first for privacy and speed. We are exploring sync options for a future update.
How do I import my bookmarks?
Go to Settings > Data. We currently support importing JSON backups. Full HTML bookmark import is coming soon!
I found a bug! Where do I report it?
Please contact support at help@whiskeydev.io.
